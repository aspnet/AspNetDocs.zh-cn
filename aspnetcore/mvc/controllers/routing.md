## <a name="understanding-iactionconstraint"></a><span data-ttu-id="f4e3f-394">了解 IActionConstraint</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f4e3f-394">Understanding IActionConstraint</span></span>

> [!NOTE]
